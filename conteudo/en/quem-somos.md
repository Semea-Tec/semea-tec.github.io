## Advisors

The project is advised by **Prof. Dr. Tiago de Oliveira** ([@tiagodeoliveira](https://github.com/tiagodeoliveira)) and co-advised by **Prof. Dr. Lauro Paulo da Silva Neto**, both from ICT/UNIFESP.

## Partner organizations

We work in partnership with organizations engaged daily in rural life:

- **Portal Sem Porteiras** — Monteiro Lobato, SP
- **Akarui** — São Luiz do Paraitinga, SP
- **Apoena** — agroecology in the Paraíba Valley

---

> SEMEA-TEC is an outreach project affiliated with the Institute of Science and Technology at UNIFESP — São José dos Campos campus. Everyone involved contributes voluntarily toward the goal of developing social technologies for family farming.
